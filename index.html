About
Michael
Mejia

I photograph people and places where identity is built through work, movement, and material. My background in sociology and corporate film production means I approach every brief the way a researcher approaches a subject—with genuine curiosity about what makes a brand's world specific, not generic. The result is campaign imagery that art buyers can actually deploy: commercially precise, editorially credible, and impossible to replicate in a studio.
